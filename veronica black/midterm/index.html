"Perishable"
a Thesis Project

Food

I am approaching my thesis project as skin as metaphor by designing a series of materials that will be an interpretation of distorted body image and eating disorders. I want to explore ways of healing from my eating disorder by developing these materials from food that will cover the body, as a second skin.

    Process

    The concept is about growing. Liquid with yeast and vinegar, silk screen frame and recycle materials. The circuit is made with nitinol wire. I tried to weave corn silk with blue jean fibers, hair, and newsprint paper. Through these experiments, I grew worms, freed them, and got accosted by the police because I look suspicious.
